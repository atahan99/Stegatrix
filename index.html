    About / Limits honesty
    

      
    
        
  • ASCII conversion is lossy and visual only. The original image cannot be reconstructed from ASCII.
    • 
        
  • Classical ciphers (Caesar, Atbash, Vigenère, XOR) are puzzle/aesthetic ciphers, not secure.
    • 
        
  • AES-GCM mode is the secure encryption option (PBKDF2-SHA256 key derivation, random salt + IV).
    • 
        
  • LSB steganography can be destroyed by compression, resizing, screenshots, or social-media re-encoding. Export as PNG only.
    • 
        
  • Zero-width stego is experimental and may be stripped by websites, editors, or messengers.
    • 
        
  • All processing happens locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
    •
